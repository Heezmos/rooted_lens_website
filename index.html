Who We Are

Delight Ventures Limited is a dynamic parent company that integrates three powerful subsidiaries: Vintech Media & Consultancy (Rooted Lens), Delight Harvest Limited, and Delight Trade Facilitation.

Our vision is to be a global leader in delivering innovation-driven solutions across industries. We believe in creativity, sustainability, and the power of technology to drive progress.
